The **multiplier** is an analog computing element capable of performing the multiplication of two quantities **x** and **y**.

**Note: The THAT multiplier unit consists of two mulipliers, one per row.**

Devision and root
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

When combining the multiplier with an open amplifier you are able to perform division and square root operations.
